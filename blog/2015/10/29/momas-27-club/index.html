MoMA's 27 Club

Oct 29th, 2015

Music popularized the idea of the 27 Club but where are the other artists who also belong?

Using the biographical data on artists provided by the MoMA I’ve extracted a non-exhaustive list of birth and death dates where available.

  • Il’ia Chashnik died from complications during surgery.
  • Robert Howlett died from arsenic and mercury poisoning as part of his photographic process. So some might say he died in a similar way to Janet and Jimi, from chemicals necessary for his art.
  • Henri Evenepoel died of Typhus.
  • Robert Adamson died from illness.
  • Roberto Ossaye died from causes I couldn’t easily determine.
  • August Macke died during the First World War.

From the dataset I also found Josef Raynefeld(Jozef Rajnfeld in Polish), but I think MoMA’s date of birth for him is incorrect. Polish Wikipedia indicates he lived to be 32.

This’ll be part of an ongoing series as I integrate other data sets in to my analysis.
